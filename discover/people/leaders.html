San Pablo City Mayors

From 1941, every mayor of San Pablo have the responsibility of the treasury of the city. And everyone of them has left their humane reforms and agendas, that contributed in making San Pablo City a safe place to live in and to invest in; and maintaining the important cultural heritage landmarks and the traditions of the people and the city.
