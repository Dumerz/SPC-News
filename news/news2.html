Mga Konsehal May kanya kanya nang Chairmanship sa Sangguniang Panglungsod

  • July 18, 2013

    • San Pablo City- Sa Isinagawang unang regular session ng Sangguniang Panlunsod nuong Hulyo 9 itinalaga na ang mga konsehal sa kani-kanilang chairmanship sa iba't- ibang committee sa pamamagitan ng botohan.

    Napiling Majority Floor Leader si Coun. Ed Dizon at kung saan pamumunuan niya ang Comm. on Rules and Privileges, Comm. on Budget and Appropriation, Comm. on Government Contract and Legal Matters,Comm. on Energy, Transportation and Telecommunications at Comm. on Education and Library Services. Si Coun. Rondel Diaz naman ay naghirang na Pro Tempore at chairman ng Comm. on NGO, Cooperative and People's Organization, Comm. on Market Slaughterhouse and Government Enterprises at Comm. on Trade, Commerce and Industry.

    Nahirang naman na Minority Floor Leader si Coun. Fernando See at chairman ng Committee on Water Resources Development. Ang Chairmanship naman ni Coun. Arnel Ticzon ay Comm. on Ways and Means, Comm. on Health and Sanitation, Committee on Engineering, Public Works and Zonification at Comm. on Housing, Land Use and Estate Development. Ang mga Commitee naman ng Welfare and Protection of Family, Children, Senior Citizen, Persons Disability; Anti Drugs Abuse, Illegal Gambling; Tourism, Culture and Arts at Information Technology and E-Commerce ay nakaatang kay Coun. Karla A. Limbo. Si Coun. Gali Galicia naman ay Committee on Human Rights at Coun. Gueren Amante ay sa Comm. of Youth and Sports Develpment.

    Si Coun. Nap Calatraba ay sa Comm. on Environment Protection and Solid Waste at Comm. on Ethics and Discipline. Ang Comm. on Police, Fire, Penology; Comm. on Barangay Affairs; Comm. on Agriculture and Economic Productivity at Comm. on Human Resources, Good Governance and Public Accountability ay kay Coun. Gener Amante.

    Ang Comm. on Labor and Employment Generation at Comm. on International Affairs ay kay Coun. Justin Colago. Si Coun. Diasdado Biglete naman ay Comm. on Urban Poor and Livelihood at Coun. Elsie Pavico ay sa Comm. on Mass Media. (CIO-SPC)
